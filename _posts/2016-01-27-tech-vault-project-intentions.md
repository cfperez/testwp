<h1><span style="font-weight: 400;">Strategic imperative</span></h1>
<span style="font-weight: 400;">To capture the collective knowledge of SVDS </span>
<h1><span style="font-weight: 400;">Business objectives</span></h1>
<ol>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Store reusable building blocks of code to aid in effective and efficient problem solving </span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Create, store, and curate pedagogical resources for training and ongoing learning</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Record and store high-level project debrief narratives in a consistent manner and in a centralized location</span></li>
</ol>
<h1><span style="font-weight: 400;">Guiding principles</span></h1>
<ul>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Low barrier to contribute but continual evolution to higher quality </span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Information that is both easy to discover and consume</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Agile - this is just at its beginning and will adapt to the future environment and latest technology as we grow</span></li>
	<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Moving towards best practices and an “SVDS way” to engagements</span></li>
</ul>
